Summer Meal Plan: a week of fresh meals to beat the heat!

Check out this meal plan and linked recipes for summer meals that require minimal to no oven and stove time. Beating the summer heat means reducing the amount of times you have to heat up your home, but you can still eat well! These fulfilling but light and fresh meals will satisfy your family and keep them cool.

Day Breakfast Lunch Dinner
Sunday Protein smoothie and avocado toast Avocado corn and bean salad with quesadilla Cheeseburgers and grilled corn
Monday Overnight oats Mediterranean chicken salad bowl with pita chips Shrimp ceviche
Tuesday Yogurt and granola bowl Italian pinwheels Chipotle chicken quinoa bowls
Wednesday Pico and potato frittata Chicken salad sandwich Appetizer night: wings, charcuterie, veggie tray, meatballs, salad
Thursday Repeat your favorite! Leftovers from dinner earlier in the week BBQ chicken sliders, coleslaw, fruit salad
