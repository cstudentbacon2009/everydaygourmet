Chicken Shawarma and Air Fryer Fries

Serves: 4–6

Tzatziki Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 large cucumber, grated and squeezed to remove excess juice
  • 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • Juice from half a lemon
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely minced or grated
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Chicken Shawarma Ingredients

  • 2.5 lb boneless chicken thighs or breasts cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1–2 tbsp fresh dill
  • 1 tbsp dried Greek oregano
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4–6 pita or naan bread
  • 1/4 red onion, sliced
  • 1 Roma tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Seasoned Air Fryer Fries Ingredients

  • 4-6 large golden potatoes, cut into fries
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Greek oregano
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a large bowl, whisk together the yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, dill, oregano, salt, and pepper. Add chicken and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours (or overnight for best flavor).
  2. Make the Tzatziki: In a small bowl, combine the grated cucumber, yogurt, dill, lemon juice, garlic, and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper. Mix well and refrigerate until ready to serve.
  3. Prepare the Fries: Toss the potato slices in olive oil and season with garlic powder, paprika, oregano, chili flakes, salt, and pepper. Air fry at 400°F (200°C) for 15–20 minutes, shaking halfway through, until golden and crispy.
  4. Grill the Chicken: Preheat your grill to medium-high. Remove the chicken from the marinade and skewer them. Grill for 5–7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked and nicely charred.
  5. Assemble the Shawarmas: Warm the pita or naan, then layer with grilled chicken, a generous spoonful of tzatziki, sliced tomato, onion, and feta. Add fries inside the wrap or serve them on the side.
  6. Serve: Wrap the shawarmas in foil or parchment if desired and enjoy immediately while warm.

Shrimp Ceviche

Serves: 4–6

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup fresh lime juice (about 8–10 limes)
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice (about 3–4 lemons)
  • 1/2 red onion, finely diced
  • 2 Roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cucumber, peeled, seeded, and diced
  • 1 jalapeño, finely minced (remove seeds for less heat)
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 avocado, diced (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortilla chips or tostadas for serving
  • 1 cup Clamato
  • Tortilla chips

Instructions

  1. Marinate the shrimp: In a glass bowl, combine shrimp with lime and lemon juice. Chill in the fridge for 30–45 minutes.
  2. Add the veggies: Stir in onion, tomatoes, cucumber, jalapeño, and cilantro. Chill another 10–15 minutes.
  3. Season and finish: Add salt and pepper. Gently fold in avocado just before serving. Optional: add clamato and hot sauce.
  4. Serve: Spoon ceviche into bowls or onto tostadas. Serve cold with tortilla chips.
