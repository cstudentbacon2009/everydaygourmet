Summer Meal Plan: Fresh Meals to Beat the Heat!

Add some of these meal that require minimal to no oven and stove time to your weekly rotation this summer! Beating the summer heat means reducing the amount of times you have to heat up your home, but you can still eat well! These fulfilling but light and fresh meals will satisfy your family and keep them cool.
