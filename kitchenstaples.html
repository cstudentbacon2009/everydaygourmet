Kitchen Staples

Like any craft, practice makes you better. When you're equipped with the right tools and ingredients, you're set up for success. Below is my list of kitchen staples I always keep on hand to make every meal a little more gourmet.


Appliances

These are my go-to tools that help me prep, cook, and serve efficiently:

  • Air fryer
  • Blender
  • Food processor
  • Cutting boards
  • Chef’s knife
  • Paring knife
  • Santoku knife
  • Salad spinner
  • Mixing bowls
  • Wooden cooking utensils
  • Sheet pan (add parchment paper for easy clean up)
  • Cast iron skillet

Essential Spices

A good spice rack is a flavor game-changer. These are my go-to seasonings to keep stocked:

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Cumin
  • Oregano
  • Dried thyme
  • Crushed red pepper
  • Chili powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • Old Bay
  • Lawry’s
  • Mrs. Dash blends
  • Cinnamon

Go-To Ingredients

I feel ready to sign up for Top Chef with the right ingredients and an extra 90 minutes on the clock. These are the ones I always keep around just in case inspiration strikes:

  • Olive and avocado oil
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, dill, basil)
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Lemon
  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Broth or stock
  • Baking soda
  • Tomato sauce
  • Soy sauce
